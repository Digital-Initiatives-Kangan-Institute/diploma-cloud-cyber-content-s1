## What this Topic must cover

The analytical setup that justifies change — diagnosing where the organisation is, where it needs to be, and the gap between. Three components:

- **C1 — Strategic alignment analysis.** Interpreting an organisation's strategic plan, extracting the objectives material to an initiative, bringing in external industry context, and identifying where the plan aligns with or diverges from industry direction.
- **C2 — Current-state synthesis.** Summarising a current ICT environment **in your own words**, focused on what's material — synthesis, not verbatim reproduction.
- **C3 — Gap analysis.** Comparing target objectives against the current state and producing a structured gap table (objective → current → desired → gap → improvement opportunity → proposed change).
