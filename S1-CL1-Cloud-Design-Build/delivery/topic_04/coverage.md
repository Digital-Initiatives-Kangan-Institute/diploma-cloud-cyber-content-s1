## What this Topic must cover

Turning the **evidence** (Topic 3) into a **recommendation and a plan a board can approve** — the final analytical third of the Business Case. After this Topic the written practice business case is **complete** (Topics 2→3→4 build §3–§5, §6–§8, §9–§11). Three components:

- **C1 — Prioritise & recommend.** Prioritising the proposed changes and selecting the recommended option, justified from the evidence (the CBA + risk from Topic 3). The decision, made and defended.
- **C2 — The action plan.** Developing the action plan that realises the recommendation: prioritised changes, an implementation schedule, standards / targets / success metrics, implementation methods, and alignment with the organisation's change-management procedure.
- **C3 — The decision requested.** Framing the specific ask to the board — what is being approved (option, plan, budget), the next steps, and how the high-risk steps route through governance.

> Components C1–C3 correspond to chunks **AT1 T13–T15**.

## Out of scope for this Topic (covered elsewhere)

- **Pitching the case** — the board presentation, oral delivery, Q&A, and the live feedback/sign-off event → **Topic 5** + **AT1 Part B**. (C3 produces the *written* decision request; Topic 5 *delivers* it. `PC 3.3`'s live approval-seeking sits there.)

- **Cloud cost / service knowledge** → **Topic 1**.
