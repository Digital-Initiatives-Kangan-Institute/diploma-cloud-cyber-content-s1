## 4. Current engagement: **LMS Cloud Migration (S1-CL1)**

YAT College is undertaking a multi-phase cloud migration of its mission-critical Learning Management System. MP Tech Solutions (MTS) has been engaged to lead the migration, with YAT ICT supporting the work and ultimately operating the migrated environment.

You — the MTS consultant assigned to this engagement — will:

1. **Analyse YAT's strategic plan, current ICT environment, and migration objectives** — produce a gap analysis, a 5-year cost-benefit analysis comparing in-house vs cloud options, and a prioritised action plan. Present to your superior for sign-off. *(S1-CL1 AT1)*
2. **Implement the cloud foundation per your approved action plan** — IAM, virtual network, compute, managed database, and a multi-tier load-balanced application with autoscaling. Document the build and obtain sign-off. *(S1-CL1 AT2)*
3. **Harden the environment for high availability** — review the on-prem LMS for single points of failure and recovery gaps, design and implement a fault-tolerant cloud architecture, convert the database to multi-AZ managed, simulate failure, and prepare the consolidated project closure pack for final sign-off via YAT's change-management procedure. *(S1-CL1 AT3)*

The engagement scenario, supporting materials, policies, procedures, and the cluster project brief all live on the **YAT Intranet** — accessible via the sign-in button above.

## 5. Your role on this engagement

You are part of the MTS team led by **Pat Lin** (MTS Senior Consultant). You report to Pat, who in turn liaises with YAT's ICT Manager (**Sam Walker**) for sign-offs. Your primary YAT-side stakeholder is Sam.
