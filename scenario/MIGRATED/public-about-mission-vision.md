## About YAT College

YAT College is a private Registered Training Organisation (RTO) with a single campus based in Cremorne, Victoria. The college offers over 50 certificate and diploma level qualifications in the Vocational Education and Training (VET) sector for a large number of subject areas including business, community services, education, information technology, health, hospitality, and many other trades.

## Mission

To provide top quality education to all students and move them into employment.

## Vision

Become one of Australia's top 5 education providers using modern technologies and teaching platforms. We want to be industry leaders in our systems and processes.
