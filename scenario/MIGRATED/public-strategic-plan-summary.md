## Strategic Business Goals

- Deliver first-class education using modern technologies
- Provide service excellence exceeding user expectations
- Implement sustainable practices
- Expand the number of locations

## Business Objectives

- Offer students new ways of learning irrespective of location
- Expand throughout Australia
- Increase student numbers by 15% annually over next 3 years
