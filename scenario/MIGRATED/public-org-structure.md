## Roles (referenced in YAT source material)

- **Senior management** — sign off on changes when risk impact requires it
- **ICT manager** — approves change requests, owns ICT-related sign-offs
- **Affected business unit heads** — secondary sign-off on changes that impact their function
- **MP Tech Solutions (MTS)** — external ICT consultancy engaged by YAT for application support and system customisation for the LMS
- **MTS-supplied consultant** — responsible for end-to-end delivery of the LMS solution; the student's superior/supervisor on this engagement
- **YAT ICT staff** — highly skilled in their current environment; lack expertise and experience with cloud technologies
- **Trainers/assessors** — qualified teaching staff
- **Students** — campus-based, full-time or part-time, trainer-led or self-directed

## Decision authority **TBD**

Derived from the documented change-management procedure (see `internal-change-management-procedure.md`). Authority thresholds below are Claude proposals — **TBD** confirm.

| Decision type | Authority |
|---|---|
| ICT change — low risk, no service interruption | ICT Manager |
| ICT change — medium risk, planned downtime within maintenance window | ICT Manager + affected business unit head |
| ICT change — high risk, mission-critical system, or interruption outside maintenance window | ICT Manager + senior management + affected business unit heads |
| Capital expenditure (per budget cycle) | Senior management within approved budget; CEO + Board for variances above 10% |
| New role / headcount addition | Director (relevant area) + CEO |
| External engagement (e.g. extending MTS scope) | ICT Manager up to capped value; CEO above that |

## Notes for the student (MTS consultant role)

- The student reports to the MTS-supplied consultant who in turn liaises with YAT's ICT Manager for sign-offs.
- The "superior" in 517 AT references is the MTS-supplied consultant.
- Final sign-off on the LMS migration is expected from the ICT Manager, with senior management notification (per the change-management procedure).
