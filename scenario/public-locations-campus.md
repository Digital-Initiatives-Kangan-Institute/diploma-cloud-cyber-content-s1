## Campus

**YAT College — Cremorne Campus**
175 Cremorne Street
Cremorne VIC 3121
Australia

YAT College operates from a single campus in Cremorne, in Melbourne's inner east. The campus houses all teaching, administration, ICT, and student services functions, and is the site where ICT needs and strategic directions are coordinated for the organisation.

The campus is also the physical location of the on-premises ICT environment described in `internal-ict-environment-overview-S1-CL1-AT1.md`, including the server room housing the LMS server and other on-prem infrastructure.

## Travel and contact

- **Public transport:** Cremorne is well served by Richmond station (Glenferrie line and most regional/V-Line services) and tram routes along Swan Street and Church Street.
- **Parking:** limited on-street parking; staff and visitor parking is provided in the campus precinct.
