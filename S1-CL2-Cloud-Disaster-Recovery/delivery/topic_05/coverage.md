# Topic 5 — Documenting & presenting the design + plan · Coverage

**Topic 5 of 10** · **AT1 content Topic** (Part A documentation + Part C presentation) · teaching source: bespoke.

This file is the **coverage spec** for the Topic.

**Depth ceiling: SKILL.** The technical content is Topics 1–4; this Topic teaches *documenting and justifying* it, then *presenting for approval*. Comes last in AT1, ahead of the Part C assessment.

---

## What this Topic must cover

Closing out the design+plan: write it up to a professional standard and present it for sign-off. Four components:

- **C1 — Documenting & justifying the design.** Document and justify the web-scale and microservice architecture changes — clear, structured, technically accurate writing in the supplied template.
- **C2 — The approval presentation.** Conduct a verbal walkthrough of the Solution Design and DR Plan with the required personnel; seek and respond to feedback during the event.
- **C3 — Lodgement & sign-off.** Lodge the plan according to organisational and legislative protocol; obtain final sign-off — the design-approval gate before implementation.
- **C4 — Knowledge-evidence Q&A.** Answer the assessor's contextual questions on the student's *own* design and plan choices — the verbal KE location.
